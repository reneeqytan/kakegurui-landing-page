Kakegurui

While Kakegurui (the anime, not the horrible remake), may seem overtly gay around the edges, it's truly a masterpiece of an anime. Despite Season 3 not being announced yet after 2 years after Season 2, many fans will be looking forward to the batshit crazy antics of Yumeko and the rest of the gay gang. I'm proud to say that after watching Kakegurui, I tried my hand at online poker (no real money involved), and I lost miserably after going all-in as a bluff. I'm still convinced that the other players cheated, perhaps by poking a hole in the virtual table and seeing my cards. Or, they may have stuck magnets on the server and did some hacker magic. Either way, I'm still convinced that I was cheated out of my money (17 million).
